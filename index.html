How to play Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

• The board is a 3×3 grid of nine smaller Tic Tac Toe games.
• Win a small game to claim that square on the big board.
• Win the big board by claiming three squares in a row.
• The cell you play in determines which small board your opponent must play in next.
• If that board is already finished, your opponent may play in any open board.

CPU Difficulty:
• Easy — mostly random moves
• Medium — thinks 3 moves ahead
• Hard — thinks 5 moves ahead with strategic evaluation
Hints
